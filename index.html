Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-05-15

Hello! My name is Ivailo Stoianov, and I am the sole developer and operator of Rant2note ("the Application"). This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you use my app. By using Rant2note, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information I Collect

  • Voice Recordings: When you use Rant2note, you can record your voice. These recordings are processed to generate text notes using AI-powered transcription and summarization.
  • Transcription & AI Data: The audio you record is sent securely to my servers and/or trusted third-party AI providers for transcription and note generation. The resulting text is stored in your account for your access.
  • Device & Usage Data: I may collect information such as your device's IP address, operating system, app version, usage statistics (e.g., which features you use, session duration), and crash logs to improve the app.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, I may collect your email address and a user ID.
  • Location Data: I do not collect precise location data. General location (country/city) may be inferred from your IP address for analytics and security.

2. How I Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the core functionality of Rant2note, including voice-to-note conversion.
  • To personalize your experience and offer relevant features.
  • To analyze usage and improve app performance.
  • To communicate with you about updates, support, or marketing (if you opt in).
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect my rights and users' safety.

3. Voice Data & AI Processing

Your voice recordings are used solely to generate text notes for you. Audio files and transcriptions may be temporarily stored on secure servers for processing. I use trusted third-party AI services (such as Google Cloud, OpenAI, or similar) for transcription and summarization. These providers process your data only as needed to provide the service and are contractually obligated to protect your privacy.

5. Data Security

6. Data Retention

I retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the service. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me.

  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and data.
